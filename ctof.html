Python Coding for Conversion of C to F and Vice-Versa


#C TO F
celcius=float(input("Enter the temperature in celcius:"))
farenheit=(1.8*celcius)+32.0
print("The temperature in farenheit is:{:.2f}".format (farenheit))

#F TO C
farenheit=float(input("Enter the temperature in Farenheit:"))
celcius=(farenheit-32)*5/9
print("The temperature in celcius is:{:.2f}".format(celcius))
