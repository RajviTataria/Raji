Paneer Bhurji


Ingredients

  1. Paneer(homemade/store bought

  2. Ginger-garlic- paste and finely chopped green chillies.

  3. Oil, spices: Cumin seeds, Red chilli powder, Turmeric, Garam masala, Coriander powder, Salt and Asafoetida.

  4. ½ cup finely chopped onion.

  5. 1 cup chopped tomatoes.

  6. 3 tatblespoons finely chopped fresh coriander.

Procedure

  1. Crumble 200-250 grams of paneer with your fingers..

  2. Heat 3 tablespoons oil(ghee/butter instead) in a frying pan or kadai. Add ½ teaspoon cumin seeds and let them crackle.

  3. Then add the ½ cup of finely chopped onions.Sauté the onions till they turn translucent and soften on medium-low heat.

  4. Then add 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chillies. Mix well and sauté for a few seconds till the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away.

  5. Add 1 cup finely chopped tomatoes. Sauté until the tomatoes soften and release oil from the sides on a medium-low flame.

  6. Now add all the spice – ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder, ½ teaspoon red chilli powder(add more or less depending on your spice tolerence.), ½ teaspoon coriander powder, ¼ to ½ teaspoon garam masala and ¼ teaspoon Asafoetida.

  7. Add the crumbled paneer and salt as per taste mix thoroughly and cook the paneer for 1 minute. Donot over cook.

  8. Switch off the flame and lastly add 3 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves.

  9. Serve with Roti or Paratha and a side of your favourite indian starter.

  10. Enjoy!!
