Fruit Sorbet


Ingredients

  1. Your favourite fruits (frozen, or fresh bought and then diced and frozen)

  2. Simple syrup as a sweetener (or agave/honey/maple syrup)

  3. And just a squeeze of lemon juice or lime juice (this isnâ€™t needed if the fruit is sour).


Procedure

  1. Add the frozen fruits to a blender and make a thick puree.

  2. Then add the simple syrup(agave/honey/maple syrup,if u want the sorbet to be a little liquidy.) or add a little powdered sugar according to your taste.

  3. Add the lemon/lime juice and mmix throughly.

  4. You can even add fruit chuncks if you want for a different texture.

  5. Freeze for several hours if not overnight.

  6. Scoop it up & Enjoy!!
