Gravy/Dry Manchurian


Ingredients (to make manchurian balls)

  1. ½ head cabbage (green or purple both are fine, remember to remove all water before you put it in the mixture.).

  2. 2 medium carrots (finely chopped).

  3. 1 medium onion (finely chopped).

  4. 1 medium green bell pepper (finely chopped).

  5. 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste

  6. 2-3 green chili peppers (like serrano. Finely minced. Use more or less based on your heat preference. Deseed for less heat)

  7. Salt to taste

  8. ½ cup unbleached all purpose flour

  9. ¼ cup corn starch

  10. oil (for frying).

Ingredients (to make sauce for dry and gravy both):

  1. 1 tbsp oil

  2. chili,garlic,ginger paste (for gravy/dry manchurian).

  3. 2 green chili peppers (like serrano, minced)

  4. 1 small green bell pepper (cut into large ¾th inch cubes/finely chopped)

  5. 2 tbsp tomato ketchup

  6. 1 tbsp hot chili sauce (use more or less based on your preference)

  7. 2 tbsp soy sauce

  8. 2 tbsp white vinegar/apple cider vinegar

  9. 4 cups vegetable stock(change aacording to the amount of gravy you want and donot add for dry manchurian).

  10. Salt to taste

  11. 4 tbsp corn starch

  12. 2 scallion/spring onions (chopped).

Procedure (to make the manchurian balls):

  1. Heat oil for frying in a wok or fryer.

  2. Place the veggies and the rest of the ingredients in the bowl and mix until a ball formed with the veggies holds together. You don't want the balls to be doughy. So, add only if necessary.

  3. Shape the mixture into ¾-inch balls and fry.

  4. Turn the manchurian balls over as needed so they are golden-brown on both sides. Remove them to a dish lined with paper towels and set aside.

Procedure (to make the gravy manchurian):

  1. To make the sauce, place the wok on high heat and when it is very hot, pour the oil in along the sides of the pan.

  2. Add the garlic and stir-fry for just 10 seconds or so, then immediately add in the bell peppers and onion. Saute just a minute, then add the sauces one by one: the ketchup, soy sauce, hot chili sauce, vinegar and the paste. Mix together quickly and then add four cups of vegetable broth to the wok and add salt according to taste. Bring to a boil.

  3. Mix the cornstarch with 4 tbsp water and add a little at a time until the sauce thickens. You don't have to use all of it, just add it until the sauce becomes as thick as your liking. Cook the sauce until it comes to a boil and becomes glossy, 2-4 minutes. Add the manchurian balls. Turn off heat and season with scallions/spring onion before serving.

  4. Serve it with Fried/Schezwan rice.

  5. Enjoy!!

Procedure (to make the dry manchurian):

  1. Now heat the sesame oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot add finely chopped ginger, garlic and green chilies.

  2. Then, add the onion and cook till they are soft and translucent or light pink.

  3. Now add ketchup, hot chilli sauce, soy sauce, vinegar and the paste and mix well.

  4. now add constarch slurry and let it simmer for 2 minutes or till you get nice thick of sauce. Make sure it is not watery. Now add salt and pepper to taste. You can add red chilli flakes too. Mix well.

  5. Now add the fried manchurian balls. Toss them and coat all the balls with that sauce. Lastly sprinkle scallions/spring onions greens and serve immediately.

  6. Serve it as a starter dish or as a side with noodles.

  7. Enjoy!!
