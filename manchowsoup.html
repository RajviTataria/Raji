Manchow Soup


Ingredients (to make for fried noodles.)

  1. 4 cup water

  2. 1 tsp salt

  3. 1 tsp oil

  4. 1 packet hakka noodles

  5. 1 tsp cornflour

  6. oil (for frying)

Ingredients (to make soup):

  1. 2 tbsp oil

  2. 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)

  3. 2 clove garlic (finely chopped)

  4. 2 chili peppers (like serrano, minced)

  5. ½ onion (finely chopped)

  6. ½ carrot (finely chopped)

  7. ¼ cabbage (finely chopped)

  8. ½ capsicum (finely chopped)

  9. 5 beans (finely chopped)

  10. 2 tbsp coriander stem (chopped)

  11. 4 cup vegetable broth/water

  12. ¾ tsp salt

  13. 2 tbsp soy sauce

  14. 2 tbsp vinegar

  15. ½ tsp pepper powder

  16. 1 tsp chilli sauce

  17. 1 tsp cornflour

  18. ¼ cup water (for slurry)

  19. 2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)


Procedure (to make fried noodles):

  1. First, in a large vessel take 4 cup water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp oil.

  2. Once the water comes to a boil, break in 1 pack hakka noodles.

  3. Boil the noodles for 5 minutes or until al dente.

  4. Drain the noodles Throughly and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process.

  5. Add 1 tsp cornflour and mix gently.

  6. Deep fry the noodles in hot oil by spreading noodles uniformly.

  7. And stir occasionally, keep the flame on medium.

  8. Flip and fry both sides until the noodles turn crisp .

  9. Finally, take crispy noodles and keep aside.

Procedure (to make the soup):

  1. First, in a wok heat 2 tbsp oil and saute the 1 inch of ginger, the 2 clove garlic and 2 chillies.

  2. And, saute ½ onion until it shrinks slightly.

  3. Now add ½ of the carrot, ¼ cabbage, ½ capsicum, 5 beans and 2 tbsp coriander stem.

  4. Stir fry them for a minute without overcooking them.

  5. Now pour the 4 cups of vegetable broth/water and add ¾ tsp salt and mix well.

  6. Boil for a couple minutes.

  7. Now add the 2 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp vinegar, ½ tsp pepper powder and 1 tsp chilli sauce and mix throughly

  8. Now in a small bowl take 1 tsp cornflour and mix with ¼ cup water.

  9. Now pour the cornflour slurry and stir well.

  10. Stir and boil until the soup thickens a little and turns glossy.

  11. Add 2 tbsp coriander and give a good mix.

  12. Serve the manchow soup topped with fried noodles.

  13. Enjoy!!
