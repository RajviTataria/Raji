Dal Khichdi


Ingredients (to make the Dal Khichdi)

  1. Basmati rice 1 cup

  2. Toor dal 1 cup

  3. Chana dal ¼ cup

  4. Masoor dal ¼ cup

  5. Ghee 2 tsp

  6. Spices: Turmeric 2 tsp, Coriander powder 1 tbsp, Red chilli powder 1 tsp, Amchur powder 1 tsp, Garam masala 1 tsp, Kasuri methi 2 tsp, Jeera (cumin seeds) 2 tsp

  7. Ginger 2 inch (finely chopped)

  8. Water 5 cups

  9. 2 Onion medium sized (chopped)

  10. Whole red chillies 2-3

  11. Garlic 2 tbsp (chopped)

  12. Green chillies 3-4 nos. (chopped)

  13. Tomatoes 2 medium sized (chopped)

  14. Salt to taste

  15. Lemon juice 1 tsp

  16. fresh coriander leaves 1 tbsp (chopped)


Ingredients (to make tempering):

  1. Ghee 2 tbsp

  2. Jeera 2 tsp

  3. Red chilli powder2 tsp

  4. Asafoetida 1 tsp

  5. 2-3 Whole red chilli.

Procedure (to make the Dal Khichdi):

  1. Rinse the Basmati rice throughly with water. Soak the rice for 10-15 minutes.

  2. Wash all the three lentils thoroughly. Soak them for ½ an hour.

  3. Add the soaked rice to boiling water and salt to taste and cook for 10-15 minutes and then strain the cooked rice.

  4. In a cooker, heat ghee and add jeera,let it splutter.

  5. Now add 1 inch of ginger and the soaked an drained lentils, add 5 cups of water, turmeric and salt to taste.

  6. Cook it on medium flame until 3-4 whistles. Switch off the flame and let the cooker depressurize naturally and give it a nice stir.

  7. In a pot heat ghee, add jeera and let it splutter, add onions and cook until they turn translucent.

  8. Now, add whole red chillies, garlic, green chillies and ginger and sauté for a minute.Then add tomatoes, salt, and all the spices. Cook until tomatoes have softened.

  9. Add the cooked dal & rice, mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes.

  10. you can also add water to adjust the consistency and then cook for another 4-5 minutes.


Procedure (to make the tempering):

  1. Heat some ghee in a pan and add jeera, red chilli powder, asafoetida, whole red chilli and mix well.

  2. Add kasuri methi and lemon juice, give it a nice stir, garnish with some fresh coriander mix and serve hot with tempering on top.

  3. Enjoy!!
