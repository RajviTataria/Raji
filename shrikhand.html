Shrikhand


Ingredients

  1. 500g Yoghurt/Dahi

  2. Saffron.

  3. 3-4 tablespoons of granulated sugar(Add less or more according to your taste).

  4. ¼-½ teaspoon cardamom/elaichi powder.

  5. Chopped nuts: Almonds, Pistachios


Procedure

  1. Take the 500g of yoghurt/dahi and put it in a muslin cloth and tie it tightly(as tight as possible) and hang it and keep a bowl at the bottom and let all the whey drain for overnight or nearly 14-17 hours.

  2. Add the saffron to water and make saffron water.

  3. After the whey has completely drained out add the saffron water.

  4. Now add 3-4 tablespoons of sugar and add ¼-½ teaspoon of cardamom/elaichi powder.

  5. Now mix it all throughly and make sure there are no lumps.

  6. You can also add seasonal fruits like mangoes to make different kinds of Shrikhand.

  7. Transfer it to the serving bowl and garnish it with the chopped nuts.

  8. Serve chill with Puri or just as a Cold Dessert.

  9. Enjoy!!
